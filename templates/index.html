FreeOpenData.com: Championing transparency as the default

At FreeOpenData the mission is to make it completely free and easy for government agencies to be transparent by default. To do this we want to make all transparency laws black and white and do a private sector take over of disclosing government records. To pay for this we aim to make the most popular open data site where we will make money from subscriptions, consulting, sponsorships, job boards, and training. To be clear we want government data to be freely accessible from many sources not just ours.

To our data portal competitors Socrata and OpenGov we thank you for your commitment to open APIs. We encourage you to make software government employees depend on operationally so you're in a position to make open data an automatic byproduct.
