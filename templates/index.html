MegaTransparency.com: Making transparency the default

MegaTransparency's mission is to efficiently publish all the world's interesting public information in one well organized place.

Important privacy notice: MegaTransparency does not sell access to data instead it publishes identifying data about non paying users including IP address, email address, and detail activity on the site. It generates revenue by offering first time users a 30 minutes grace period to purchase a $19.99/month subscription which makes their user and activity data private. The rationale for this privacy policy is to make information freely available while having a revenue model and promoting transparency around the access and use of information.
